Abstract

The field of video generation has advanced rapidly with the emergence of di- verse open-source and commercial models. However, the limited controllability in generating videos that precisely align with user requirements is still a persist- nent challenge. This work focuses on the crucial aspect of subject-consistency in video generation. Existing methods, whether single-subject-oriented (like ID- Animator and MovieGen) or multi-subject-extended (ConceptMaster, Video Al- chemist, PHANTOM), exhibit shortcomings in consistency and quality. VACE, a multi-modal video-generation model, also struggles with ID-consistency due to excessive training tasks. To address these issues, we introduce HunyuanCus- tom, a novel multi-modal video-generation model built upon the HunyuanVideo framework, with a focus on ID-consistent generation. By integrating a Llava-based text-image fusion module and an image ID enhancement module, it enables the generation of videos corresponding to given image IDs under text guidance. Fur- thermore, it extends its capabilities to audio- and video-conditioned generation, significantly enhancing its versatility. For audio-driven single-subject generation, we propose Audio-Net which achieves multi-level audio-video feature alignment. For video-conditioned operations, we develop a latent-alignment module and a face-feature-aligned video patchfy module to enable efficient video information in- jection. Extensive experiments on single-and multi-subject consistency generation demonstrate that HunyuanCustom outperforms both open-source and commercial models in ID-consistency, visual realism, and video-text alignment. Additional evaluations on audio-driven generation, video object replacement, and object ad- dition further validate the superiority of our approach, showcasing its potential to improve controllable video generation.

Video-driven Video Generation

Based on subject customization, HunyuanCustom also supports using a video as the input to replace or add specified objects. Specifically, given a video with its specified actions and environment, and then given an image with specified ID information, HunyuanCustom can replace the specified object in the video with the ID specified in the image.
