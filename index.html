The field of video generation has advanced rapidly with the emergence of diverse open-source and commercial models. However, the limited controllability in generating videos that precisely align with user requirements is still a persistnent challenge. This work focuses on the crucial aspect of subject-consistency in video generation. Existing methods, whether single-subject-oriented (like ID-Animator and MovieGen) or multi-subject-extended (ConceptMaster, Video Alchemist, PHANTOM), exhibit shortcomings in consistency and quality. VACE, a multi-modal video-generation model, also struggles with ID-consistency due to excessive training tasks. To address these issues, we introduce HunyuanCustom, a novel multi-modal video-generation model built upon the HunyuanVideo framework, with a focus on ID-consistent generation. By integrating a Llava-based text-image fusion module and an image ID enhancement module, it enables the generation of videos corresponding to given image IDs under text guidance. Furthermore, it extends its capabilities to audio- and video-conditioned generation, significantly enhancing its versatility. For audio-driven single-subject generation, we propose Audio-Net which achieves multi-level audio-video feature alignment. For video-conditioned operations, we develop a latent-alignment module and a face-feature-aligned video patchfy module to enable efficient video information injection. Extensive experiments on single-and multi-subject consistency generation demonstrate that HunyuanCustom outperforms both open-source and commercial models in ID-consistency, visual realism, and video-text alignment. Additional evaluations on audio-driven generation, video object replacement, and object addition further validate the superiority of our approach, showcasing its potential to improve controllable video generation.

Method

We propose HunyuanCustom, a multi-modal, conditional, and controllable generation model centered on subject consistency, built upon the Hunyuan Video generation framework. It enables the generation of subject-consistent videos conditioned on text, images, audio, and video inputs. Specifically, HunyuanCustom introduces an image-text fusion module based on LLaVA to facilitate interaction between images and text, allowing identity information from images to be effectively integrated into textual descriptions. Additionally, an image ID enhancement module is proposed, which concatenates image information along the temporal axis and leverages the video model's efficient temporal modeling ability to enhance subject identity throughout the video. To support conditional injection of audio and video, HunyuanCustom designs distinct injection mechanisms for each modality, which are effectively disentangled with the identity condition module. HunyuanCustom ultimately achieves decoupled control over image, audio, and video conditions, demonstrating great potential in subject-centric multi-modal video generation.

Video-Driven Video Customization

Based on subject customization, HunyuanCustom also supports using a video as the input to replace or add specified objects. Specifically, given a video with its specified actions and environment, and then given an image with specified ID information, HunyuanCustom can replace the specified object in the video with the ID specified in the image.
